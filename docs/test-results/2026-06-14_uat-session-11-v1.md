### Bug uncovered + fixed live: worker crash on `gevent.Timeout`
The worker crashed mid manifest fetch (`steam_worker.died reason=stdout_closed`) with no diagnostics. Fixed in two commits on PR #155:
1. Drain + capture worker stderr (instrumentation + latent pipe-fill stall fix) ‚Äî captured the real fault.
2. `gevent.Timeout` (a `BaseException`) from a slow CDN depot escaped every `except Exception` and killed the worker. Now caught defense-in-depth (handler ‚Üí retryable `SteamCDNTimeout`; dispatch loop guards every handler). Re-validated live: app 340 (the crasher) now fetches cleanly and the worker serves library_sync‚Üímanifest‚Üíprefill‚Üívalidate in sequence with zero deaths.
